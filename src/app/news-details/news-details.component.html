ATU Alumni Association Launches New Mentorship Program

We're excited to announce the launch of our new alumni mentorship program, connecting current students with experienced graduates for career guidance and professional development.

The program will focus on career counseling, skill development, and networking opportunities across various industries. Over 200 alumni have already signed up as mentors, representing fields from technology and engineering to business, healthcare, and the arts.

Program Highlights

  • One-on-one mentorship matching based on career interests
  • Monthly virtual networking events
  • Career development workshops
  • Industry-specific guidance and advice
  • Internship and job placement assistance

"This program represents our commitment to supporting both current students and our alumni community," said Dr. Sarah Johnson, Director of Alumni Relations. "By connecting these two groups, we're creating powerful networks that benefit everyone involved."
